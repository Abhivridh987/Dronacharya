About Dronacharya

Dronacharya is a free video streaming platform dedicated to providing comprehensive BTech educational videos for KTU University students. We aim to support and empower students by providing quality video lectures covering all semesters from S1 to S8 — made by students, for students.

What We Do

  • Curated syllabus-based video lectures verified by experienced educators and top-performing students.
  • Comprehensive video coverage for all subjects from Semester 1 to Semester 8 under KTU curriculum.
  • Subject-wise, semester-wise, and department-wise organization for easy navigation.
  • High-quality video content covering theory, problem-solving, and practical demonstrations.
  • Free access to all educational videos for every BTech student.

Why It Matters

Academic pressure, time constraints, and inaccessible quality educational videos are real challenges. Dronacharya ensures no student is left behind — providing free access to comprehensive video lectures that help students master their subjects and succeed in their BTech journey.

Our Mission

Just as Arjun became one of the greatest warriors under the teaching of Guru Dronacharya, we aim to help every BTech student excel in their academics through quality video education. Our platform is built by passionate educators, mentors, and students who believe in making quality education accessible to all.
